Understand the Indian pen industry landscape and identify strategic opportunities for Unomax to build strong brand positioning in a commoditized, low-involvement category.

Reynolds 045, Cello Gripper = legendary school pens. Child's first pen choice = 20+ year loyalty.

CASE STUDIES: HOW COMPETITORS SOLVED THE CHALLENGE

REYNOLDS: Trust + Availability

Strategy: 55K+ outlets + TV advertising + Sachin = "Default Pen"

Why It Worked: Converted commodity into ritual; 045 model became legendary

Lesson: Over-invest in distribution + simple messaging + cultural icons

CELLO: Emotion + Companionship

Strategy: "Pen India Trusts" + MS Dhoni + Kavishala poetry campaign

Why It Worked: Reframed as "journey companion," not functional tool

Lesson: One sharp line + cultural hooks + consistency = top-of-mind

LINC PENTONIC: Premiumization

Strategy: Sub-brand design-led escape; ‚Çπ10 price, premium positioning

Why It Worked: Carved niche without leaving mass category

Lesson: Design + packaging + media = signal premium at low price
